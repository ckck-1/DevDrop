Welcome to DevDrop 💧
DevDrop is where high-growth startups and elite developers meet without the noise. We’ve stripped away the long applications and boring job boards, replacing them with AI-powered matching and direct, real-time communication.

🚀 Getting Started
1. Build Your Profile
Developers: Sync your stack. Tell us what you actually build with (React, Node, Go, Rust, etc.). Our AI uses this to find roles where you’ll actually thrive.

Startups: Post your mission. Don't just list requirements—tell developers why your project matters and what the vibes are like.

2. The "Live Feed"
Forget scrolling through thousands of irrelevant posts. Your feed is AI-ranked.

Roles that perfectly match your skills appear at the top.

See salary ranges, tech stacks, and company vibes at a glance.

3. One-Click Applications (Apply via DM)
We don't do "Submit PDF." When you find a role you love, hit Apply via DM.

This immediately opens a direct conversation between the developer and the startup founder or hiring lead.

No "black hole" applications. You’re in the chat immediately.

4. Real-Time Messaging
Talk shop in real-time. Our messaging system supports:

Instant Notifications: Know the second someone responds.

Typing Indicators: See when the other person is active.

Direct Access: No middle-men, no recruiters—just builders talking to builders.

💡 Pro-Tips
Be Specific: The AI likes detail. The more specific you are about your tech stack or project needs, the better your matches will be.

Keep it Casual: Since DevDrop is chat-first, don't feel the need to be overly formal. Start the conversation like you're meeting at a hackathon.

Check the Status: Jobs are marked as Open, Paused, or Closed in real-time so you never waste time applying to a dead role.
